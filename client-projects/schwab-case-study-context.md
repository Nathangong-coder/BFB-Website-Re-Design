# Case Study Context: BFB x Charles Schwab

## Project Overview
**Client:** Charles Schwab
**Goal:** Educate high-net-worth (HNW) clients on alternative investments, specifically focusing on the future of cryptocurrency in wealth management.

## The Problem (from SOW)
Charles Schwab's HNW clients are increasingly interested in alternative investments but are often deterred by the extreme complexity of these products. There is a significant gap between client interest and their ability to make informed, appropriate portfolio choices due to a lack of synthesized, institutional-grade educational resources.

## The Objective
To demystify cryptocurrency and related alternative assets by providing a structured, professional education framework. The goal is to translate technical complexity into strategic value, allowing advisors to justify inclusion and clients to understand risk/reward profiles.
